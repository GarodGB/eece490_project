AI Academic Advisor

Answers use your real profile: completed courses (with semesters), GPA, recommendations engine, bottlenecks, and planner. Ask about prerequisites, next courses, or your timeline.

Your AI Advisor can help with:
  • Recommendation summary: “Why were these courses recommended?”, “Which option is safest for my GPA?”
  • GPA feasibility: “Can I reach my target GPA?”, “Is my target realistic this semester?”
  • Course reasoning: “Is EECE210 unlocked?”, “How difficult is MATH201 for me?”
  • Strengths & weaknesses: “What are my weak areas?”, “Which course areas fit me best?”
